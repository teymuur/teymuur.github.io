Values


Supporting open source is a big part of my journey, using free-to-use platforms that believe in sharing and being open. Going for open source not only means working together with different people but also making sure that everyone can easily see and use the ideas, creating a diverse and friendly digital space. I believe that information must be shared among others people like Aaron Swartz helped me adopt these values.

Backing free speech is not just a choice; it's like planting a seed for ongoing personal and collective learning. Participating in platforms that encourage open discussions creates a lively space where individuals can refine their perspectives, explore new ideas, and benefit from a variety of voices, fostering a big community of people who appreciate expressing themselves freely.

Picking platforms that stand for open source shows a promise to make a digital world that's easy to get into and well connected. These open platforms fit right in with the idea of free talk, creating a culture of coming up with new ideas, being welcoming to everyone, and sticking together through the changes in how we talk and share.
